PUP OVAL

It consists of a well-maintained
oval track with standard dimensions
for races and a high-quality synthetic
surface. Surrounding the track, there
are designated areas for field events,
providing athletes with the necessary
space and equipment for various
disciplines.

SWIMMING POOL

It features a large, Olympic-sized
pool with multiple lanes for competitive
swimming and training. The facility
is equipped with modern amenities,
including diving boards, starting blocks,
and spectator seating, creating a
welcoming and supportive environment
for both swimmers and spectators.

LAGOON

is a vibrant space filled
with various food stalls,
catering to a diverse range
of tastes and preferences.
The lively atmosphere, combined
with delicious food options,
creates a social hub where
people can relax, socialize,
and satisfy their hunger cravings.
